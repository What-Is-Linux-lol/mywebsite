About Nathan Jenkinson

Welcome!

Hi! My name is Nathan Jenkinson, and I’m a dedicated college student pursuing a degree in Cyber Defense. My journey in the world of cybersecurity began with a strong curiosity for how computers and networks function—and grew into a lifelong passion for sharing knowledge and helping others embrace technology responsibly.

Why I’m Here

I created this platform to share everything I’ve learned and taught myself about cyber defense and computer technologies. From essential concepts to advanced topics, I’m excited to foster a space where individuals can grow their skills, develop their confidence, and stay safe in the digital world. Cyber hygiene is not just a necessity—it’s a habit I want to encourage in everyone.

What I Offer

  • Cybersecurity Tips: Learn how to protect your personal and professional digital spaces with safe practices.
  • Tech Tutorials: Explore new ways of computing, tackling unfamiliar projects, and solving real-world problems with technology.
  • Beginner to Advanced Learning: Whether you’re new to cyber or a seasoned tech enthusiast, I aim to offer something valuable for everyone.
  • Computer Basics: I’m not just about cybersecurity! I’ll also share tips and insights on general computing for anyone looking to master their devices.

My Mission

I believe in empowering others to explore, learn, and create with technology. Through this blog and my resources, I want to break down complex topics into manageable lessons and ignite a passion for lifelong learning. Technology is for everyone—and I’m here to make it accessible, understandable, and exciting.

Fun Facts About Me

  • I love experimenting with new programming languages and tools in my free time.
  • My favorite cybersecurity challenge? Reverse engineering and penetration testing—it’s like solving a digital puzzle!
  • When I’m not at my computer, you’ll likely find me exploring nature or enjoying a good science fiction book.
